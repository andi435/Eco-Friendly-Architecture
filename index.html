Our Services

Energy Cost Reduction Strategy

We develop comprehensive strategies to minimize energy costs for new constructions and renovations. Our process begins with an in-depth consultation to understand your specific energy needs. If necessary, we analyze sensor data to gain deeper insights. Based on this information, we create a tailored strategy that outlines effective methods to significantly reduce your energy consumption and costs.

Sustainable Architectural Design and 3D Drafting

Our team specializes in creating environmentally conscious designs that significantly reduce energy consumption and carbon footprints. We integrate sustainable materials and innovative design strategies to help you build spaces that are both beautiful and eco-friendly. Our services include detailed 3D drafting, ensuring precise and accurate representations of your sustainable building projects.

Holistic Energy Modeling and Analysis

We take a holistic approach to energy modeling, considering not just local weather patterns, but also building design, occupancy patterns, and system efficiencies. Our detailed energy models incorporate multiple factors to provide a comprehensive analysis of your building's energy performance. This integrated approach results in actionable insights that optimize energy use across all aspects of your project.

Energy-Efficient Design Solutions

Our design solutions can reduce your energy bills by up to 50% through passive energy designs and other sustainable approaches. We focus on creating spaces that are not only cost-effective, environmentally responsible and enjoyable as well.

Optimized Building Envelope

Design, implement and ensure minimal heat transfer and optimal thermal comfort year-round.

Passive Solar Design

Maximize natural heating and cooling, reducing reliance on mechanical systems and costs.

Ventilation and Air Quality

State-of-the-art ventilation systems, to maintain excellent indoor air quality while minimizing energy loss.

Airtight Construction & Retrofit

Eliminate drafts and uncontrolled air leakage, significantly improve energy efficiency and indoor comfort.

Renewable Energy Integration

Seamlessly integrate renewable energy systems (solar panels, geothermal heat pumps, or wind turbines), reduce reliance on grid electricity.

Water Conservation

Minimize water consumption and promote sustainable water usage.
