Our Design Tools

At Verdi Studio, we leverage industry-leading software to deliver exceptional designs and solutions. Our team is certified and experienced in using these powerful tools to bring your vision to life.

Rhino 7.0

Rhinoceros (Rhino) is our go-to solution for precision 3D modeling. With its versatile NURBS-based engine, we create complex surfaces and organic forms with mathematically exact precision. Rhino 7.0 allows us to:

  • Create unlimited free-form 3D geometry
  • Develop precise architectural and product designs
  • Generate parametric modeling through Grasshopper
  • Export to any industry-standard format
  • Rapidly prototype concepts and iterations

Autodesk Revit

Our Building Information Modeling (BIM) workflows are powered by Revit, enabling us to deliver intelligent 3D building models with coordinated documentation. With Revit, we provide:

  • Complete architectural, structural, and MEP documentation
  • Parametric components that adapt to design changes
  • Multi-discipline coordination to reduce conflicts
  • Accurate quantity takeoffs and material scheduling
  • Real-time 3D visualization of building systems

Twin Motion

When it comes to immersive visualization, we rely on Twin Motion to transform architectural models into stunning real-time experiences. This powerful engine enables us to:

  • Create photorealistic renderings with minimal setup time
  • Produce engaging walkthrough animations and presentations
  • Implement dynamic lighting conditions and seasonal changes
  • Populate scenes with lifelike characters and vegetation
  • Generate VR-ready experiences for client immersion

Rhino inside Revit

We bridge the gap between conceptual design and documentation by leveraging Rhino inside Revit. This innovative integration allows us to:

  • Use Grasshopper's parametric modeling directly within the Revit environment
  • Seamlessly convert complex Rhino geometry into native Revit elements
  • Maintain design intent throughout the BIM process
  • Create custom adaptive components for unique architectural features
  • Develop algorithmic solutions for complex design challenges
